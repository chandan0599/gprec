Consultancy Cell

The college provides consultancy services across engineering disciplines through experienced, qualified faculty. Services include materials testing and project advice. All laboratories feature modern, annually calibrated equipment upgraded to meet industry standards.

Civil Engineering Department

Civil engineering has provided consultancy for two decades, through the following laboratories.

Materials Testing Laboratory: Fine and coarse aggregate testing, cement quality assessment, brick testing, steel reinforcement tension testing, concrete compressive strength evaluation, concrete mix design with accelerated curing, non-destructive building quality assessment, and structural design consultancy for various structure types.

Soil Testing Laboratory: Undisturbed and distributed soil sample analysis, sieve analysis, liquid/plastic limit determination, compaction testing (standard and heavy), consolidation testing, hydrometer analysis, swelling pressure assessment, and shear and unconfined compression testing.

Transportation Engineering Laboratory: Bitumen testing (penetration, softening point, flash/fire point, ductility, solubility, specific gravity), coarse aggregate analysis, mix design for various bituminous types, and field testing via CBR method and pavement deflection analysis.

Environmental Engineering Laboratory: Solid composition analysis (suspended, dissolved, volatile, fixed, settleable), turbidity and pH measurement, hardness and alkalinity assessment, dissolved oxygen/BOD/COD determination, coagulant dosing optimization, and chlorine demand evaluation.

Additional Services: Surveying for land measurement, site plan and contour map preparation, and mineral and rock identification.

Major Clients: State Government (Roads & Buildings, Panchayathi Raj, Irrigation, RWS, AP Transco), Central Government (Central Railway, Telecom, CPWD, CWC), and regional private-sector contractors in irrigation, construction, and railway departments.

Mechanical Engineering Department

3D plastic component printing, natural frequency and mode shape analysis, UTM tensile/compression/flexural strength testing, composite wear and impact testing, chemical composite analysis, vacuum bag and hand layup molding, hardness testing, stir casting for metal matrix composites, CNC lathe/milling operations, and tool force measurement.

Additional services: Pressure gauge and water meter testing/calibration, 2D/3D CAD drawing and analysis, CNC code generation, thermal property evaluation, casting pattern preparation, industrial work/time studies, and non-conventional energy device installation support.

Electrical Engineering Department

Testing available for megger (0-100mW), voltmeters (0-600V AC/DC), ammeters (0-20A AC/DC), multimeters, tong testers, solid insulation (0-60kV), liquid insulation (0-60kV), and insulation testing (2.5kV). CSE and ECE faculty offer custom software development and circuit design services.
